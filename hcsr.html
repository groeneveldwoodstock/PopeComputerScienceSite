About Honors Computer Science Research

Honors Computer Science Research uses modules from CodeHS to teach App Development, Game Development, Web Development, and Portfolio Creation. All work done in class is posted on the course calendars. Links to calendars are posted below. Most calendar links require access to the CodeHS platform and enrollment in the course. Parents can use the calendar to see what was assigned on a given day. Students can access their assignments through the calendar.
